OUR PARTNERS

These businesses, companies and organizations are important to the running of our rescue. They help us by donating their time and resources, which allows us to keep our operating costs as low as possible. This in turn allows us to help more animals in need find wonderful forever homes.

Pet Valu Website

Pet Valu located at 15 Sage Hill Ct NW allows us to host adoption events every month. We also keep a special kitty in their cat condo. In addition, they collect donations from the public for us. They have been so supportive of our efforts to find forever homes for our animals.

Dogs Rule Pet Grooming Website

Dogs Rule Pet Grooming is located at 4708 26 Ave SW, Calgary. All of our Rescue dogs enjoy a complete spa treatment before they are adopted. They want to look their best for their new family.

Alexis Peters

Alexis Peters volunteers her skills as a professional photographer to come and take stunningly beautiful pictures of our foster animals. Her skilled photographs help potential adopters to see the inner beauty of our animals, not just their outer beauty. Many of the photographs featured on this website were taken by Alexis for us, and we are grateful for the time and skills that she shares with us.

Petsmart Website

PETSMART located at 321 61st Ave SW (across from Chinook Mall) this store has been a long time supporter of ours. They host monthly adoption events for our animals and allow us space for a cat in their cat room. They collect donations for us and invite us to participate in all of their major adoption events.
