One Time Donation

We welcome donations of any amount. As the person making the donation you can make it a general donation or you can direct the money to be spent in a specific way. An example is making a monetary donation that is ear marked for crates.

In Memoriam

Sometimes people like to make a donation in the name of a beloved pet who has passed away. Other times people make a donation in the name of a family member or friend who was an animal lover. We gratefully accept both forms of donation.

Ongoing Donations

Sponsorship

Sponsor an Animal

Sponsor the animal of your choice. $15 per month helps to offset the cost of food and vet care for a dog. $10 a month helps with the cost of food and vet care for a cat. You will be provided with pictures and monthly updates on your animal. Additionally your name will be honored on the animals profile.

Sponsor a special needs animal

This can be a monthly amount of your choice. You can also chose to pay for a special items require by the animals. This can be items like prescription foods, medications or veterinarian services.

Sponsor an Event

Individuals, families or even companies are welcome to sponsor one of our special events. You can either sponsor an upcoming event, or we can work with you to plan a new event. If you are interested in sponsor an event please email info@calgaryanimalrescue.com
