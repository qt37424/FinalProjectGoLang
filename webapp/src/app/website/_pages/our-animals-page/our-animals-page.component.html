About our Animals

As a rescue group we are dedicated to helping animals in need, and trying to prevent more animals from needing to be rescued. With this goal in mind all of our animals are spayed or neutered prior to their adoption. Even our puppies and kittens come spayed or neutered. Additionally all of our animals will be up to date on all vaccinations, microchipped and have been treated for parasites. Animals requiring additional veterinary care will receive it prior to being adopted. If the condition is of a chronic nature we will of course inform potential adopters.

All of our animals will have spent a minimum of 10 days in foster care. This allows us to get an idea of the animals personality and what kind of family they would best suit. Animals will not be placed on our adoptable list until they have completed a full assessment. Once you have decided that you have an interest in adopting one of our animals, please fill out an adoption application. Once we receive the application our adoption coordinator will contact you to discuss your application. This is also the time when a meet and greet can be set up between potential adopters and the animal in question.
