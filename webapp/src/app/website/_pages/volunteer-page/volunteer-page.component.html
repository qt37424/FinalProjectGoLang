VOLUNTEER

Interested in Volunteering with us?

Volunteers are the back bone of our organization. Without our volunteers we would not be able to help as many animals as we do each year. Our volunteers help to facilitate our adoption events, our charity casinos and all of our special events. They help us to transport our animals to grooming sessions and to adoption events. Some of our volunteers provide opportunities for our dogs to be socialized and exercised. They can also help at foster homes if need.
Volunteering is a rewarding experience for any individual. It allows you to give back to your community and spend time with people who share your interests and values. Volunteering with Calgary Animal Rescue Society also allows you to help our animals transition from unwanted and abandoned animals to loving family pets. You can volunteer as an individual or as a group, we welcome any and all help with our rescue.

What experience do I need?

We welcome volunteers of all experiences! The Volunteer Coordinator will be here to help with any questions or concerns you may have so please don’t hesitate to ask


How much time do I need to commit?

Although we don’t expect anyone to commit a certain amount of hours per week, we would like to see you at a volunteer event at least once every 3-4 months. We’re a very friendly organization at Calgary Animal Rescue Society, we like to get to know our volunteers! You get what you put in and it is your learning experience, after-all.


Can I use my volunteer time as credit for school or work community service requirements?

Absolutely! Please let us know in the comment section or through an email if this is the case. Reference letters are available upon request, but require minimum 2 week’s notice.


How old do you have to be to volunteer?

We value volunteers of all ages. However, for the safety of our animals as well as our volunteers, our volunteer positions have age restrictions. Youths aged 15-17 may volunteer without a parent, but under the supervision of another adult.

Volunteering will not change the world, but it can save an animals life. The people who volunteer with animal rescues are our heroes. A volunteers heart comes from the love that is seen with an animals eyes, making them rare and priceless … volunteer, feel that love.
